The Challenge Farmers Face

The current crop insurance claim process is broken. It's a major roadblock for millions, undermining the goal of "Build for the Nation".

Complex Forms

Farmers struggle with confusing paperwork often not in their local language.

Long Delays

Manual processing leads to frustratingly long waits for claim settlement.

Lack of Transparency

No clear way to track claim status, leading to uncertainty and anxiety.

How It Works: Powered by inya.ai

Our agent automates the entire end-to-end decision process through five intelligent steps.

1. Conversational Intake

inya.ai's NLU understands local languages, guiding farmers through simple questions to gather claim details.

2. Document Processing

The agent uses Computer Vision to read uploaded documents, extract data, and validate their authenticity and clarity.

3. Autonomous Form Filling

It takes the verified data and autonomously populates the complex official PMFBY insurance forms in the background.

4. API Orchestration

inya.ai securely connects to government and insurance APIs to submit the completed application without human intervention.

5. Real-time Analytics

The agent provides a live feed to the dashboard, constantly analyzing the claim pipeline to offer real-time status and KPIs.

Result

A complex, weeks-long process is reduced to a simple, transparent, 15-minute conversation.
