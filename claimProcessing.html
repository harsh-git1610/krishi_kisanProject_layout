The Challenge Farmers Face

The current crop insurance claim process is broken. It's a major roadblock for millions, undermining the goal of "Build for the Nation".

Complex Forms

Farmers struggle with confusing paperwork often not in their local language.

Long Delays

Manual processing leads to frustratingly long waits for claim settlement.

Lack of Transparency

No clear way to track claim status, leading to uncertainty and anxiety.

Our Solution: The Agentic AI Approach

We leverage inya.ai to build an autonomous agent that handles the entire end-to-end process, making it simple, fast, and transparent.
